Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

What's included in the hosting?

All our plans include 1 year of premium hosting with 99.9% uptime guarantee, free SSL certificate, and daily backups. After the first year, hosting can be renewed at ₹2,000/month for Starter and Professional plans, or ₹2,000/week for Enterprise plans.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll simply charge the difference between your current plan and the upgraded one, prorated based on your usage.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options. You can pay 50% upfront and 50% upon completion, or we can set up monthly installments for larger projects. Contact us to discuss custom payment arrangements.

How long does development take?

Typical timelines are 1-2 weeks for Starter sites, 2-4 weeks for Professional sites, and 4-8 weeks for Enterprise solutions. These timelines depend on project complexity and how quickly we receive content/assets from you.
